Dreamrite Solution/Services

Services

... Training & Employee Development through Innovative Learning Solutions......

We are passionate about enabling organizational and personal learning for visible results. We offer robust training courses with a focus on relevance and actual translation into the working environment. Our mission is to provide business like yours with quality training which address not only your immediate need, but to cover the long term needs for on-going improvement for your work teams and for individuals.

We deliver skill enhancement courses for businesses, government organizations, and non-profit entities of any size. We offer both organization specific training and generalized courses for a variety of job skills, including training for Human Resources Management, Soft Skills, Management& Supervisory Skills, Leadership and functional business knowledge. We offer on-site courses, open classes and have a robust on-line learning solution to meet your needs.

Training interventions are offered in four main modes:

  • Instructor Led In house Courses
  • Online Learning
  • 'Blended' Learning
  • 'Bite sized' workshops

The techniques used to deliver our trainings are anchored on - accelerated learning, cognitive training methods, practical demonstrations, tests and learning evaluation.This combination of techniques is based on our in-depth understanding of adult learning techniques. Our focus in designing our programs has been rapid translation of knowledge gained into the workplace. We adapt selected content to address specific business issues, emphasizing practical application to real situations. We find that this approach significantly enhances the ability of participants to quickly translate learning into action at work.

All courses are delivered using evaluation and practical demonstrations to ensure internalization of concepts taught. Programs available are highlighted our Course Brochures.
